What is Deepwork4humans?

This blog is a humble attempt to document the process of translating the ideas and inspiration drawn from the book Deep work into my real working environment as a software engineer. Since I’m now at a point where I come back to work after a short period of leave. I used some of the time to reflect and came with a resolution to be even more intentional and focused on working with a deep work mindset. Trying to achieve the best results by working focused during my workday and resting and spending time with my family during the resting hours. (A.K.A - work life balance)
